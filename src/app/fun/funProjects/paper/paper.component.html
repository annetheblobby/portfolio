Paper: A Light Installation

Context
A passion project

Themes
Education, Tangible design, Sustainability

Materials
Recycled Paper, LED light system

This LED-powered light installation takes the viewer through the history of paper through a connected length of paper cut designs. The series of depicted events, from its ancient origins in China to the crusades, the paper mills, and modern usages are carved along a spiral representing the tree's age rings.

The entirety of its paper construction is from recycled paper. The sides of the installation imitate the bark of a tree and was constructed with a collage technique. The LED fixture was chosen to minimize energy consumption and provide another way the viewer can interact with the piece. The lights are connected to a remote that changes the light dimness and color to the user's preference.
